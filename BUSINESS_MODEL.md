# H3M4 Business Model Canvas

## 1. Value Propositions
*   **For Enterprises (Banks/FinTech):**
    *   **Proactive Defense:** Access to 0-day vulnerability intelligence before exploitation.
    *   **Collective Immunity:** "Herd immunity" for the sector - if one bank is targeted, all subscribers are warned.
    *   **Compliance Automation:** Real-time mapping of threats to RBI/SEBI/GDPR compliance standards.
    *   **Operational Efficiency:** Automated patch management signals reduce SOC workload.
*   **For Law Enforcement (Police):**
    *   **Attribution & Evidence:** "Court-Ready Evidence Packages" that link technical logs to researcher analysis.
    *   **Speed:** Reduces investigation time from months to minutes using "Attached Intelligence".
    *   **Forensic Integrity:** Immutable chain of custody on a ledger.
*   **For Security Researchers:**
    *   **Immediate Monetization:** Paid for valid signals, not just when a patch is issued.
    *   **Reputation:** Gamified "Sovereign ID" system for elite status.
    *   **Legal Safety:** A governed, authorized channel for disclosure.

## 2. Customer Segments
*   **Primary (Revenue Driver):** Enterprise CISO Offices (Banking, FinTech, Insurance, Critical Infrastructure).
*   **Secondary (Strategic):** Government Agencies (Cyber Crime Units, National Security, Regulators).
*   **Tertiary (Supply Side):** Elite Security Researchers (Vetted, Top 5%).

## 3. Channels
*   **Direct Sales:** Enterprise sales team targeting CISOs and CTOs.
*   **Government Tenders:** Bidding for national cyber-defense infrastructure contracts.
*   **Researcher Community:** Capture the Flag (CTF) events, conferences (BlackHat, DEFCON), and invite-only networks.

## 4. Customer Relationships
*   **Automated (Self-Service):** For Researcher signal submission and basic Enterprise dashboards.
*   **Dedicated Support:** For Enterprise "War Room" access and custom integration.
*   **Strategic Partnership:** With Law Enforcement for custom forensic tool development.

## 5. Revenue Streams
1.  **Enterprise SaaS Subscription:**
    *   **Tier 1 (Monitor):** $50k/year - Read-only access to risk signals.
    *   **Tier 2 (Defense):** $120k/year - Automated integration + patchy priority.
    *   **Tier 3 (Sovereign):** $200k+/year - Full "War Room" access + dedicated analyst support.
2.  **Institutional Licensing:**
    *   Multi-year government contracts for the "Tactical Investigation Suite" (Police Portal).
3.  **Marketplace Transaction Fee:**
    *   10% "Handshake Fee" on all bounties/rewards paid to researchers through the platform.

## 6. Key Resources
*   **The H3M4 Vault:** Secure, encrypted platform infrastructure.
*   **"B2R" Engine:** Proprietary Byte-to-Record AI for forensic reporting.
*   **Elite Researcher Network:** The human capital providing the intelligence.
*   **Regulatory Knowledge Base:** Real-time mapping of laws to technical threats.

## 7. Key Activities
*   **Signal Verification:** Validating researcher submissions (The "Governor" Node).
*   **Intelligence Correlation:** Mapping threats to specific enterprise assets.
*   **Platform Governance:** Maintaining trust, vetting researchers, and ensuring legal compliance.
*   **Ecosystem Growth:** Recruiting top researchers and onboarding key financial institutions.

## 8. Key Partnerships
*   **Regulatory Bodies (RBI/SEBI):** To endorse H3M4 as a compliance standard.
*   **Forensic Labs:** To validate the "Court-Ready" nature of the evidence.
*   **Cloud Providers (AWS/Azure):** For secure, scalable infrastructure hosting.

## 9. Cost Structure
*   **Platform Development:** Engineering costs for the Vault and B2R engine.
*   **Verification Operations:** Costs associated with the "Governor" node validation process.
*   **Legal & Compliance:** Maintaining high-tier legal standing for handling sensitivity data.
*   **Sales & Marketing:** Enterprise sales cycles and community building.
