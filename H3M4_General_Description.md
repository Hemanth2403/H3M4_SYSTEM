# H3M4 Ecosystem: General Project Overview

## 🚀 Project Abstract
**H3M4** (pronounced "Hema") is a next-generation **Decentralized Cyber-Intelligence & Evidence Ecosystem**. It bridges the critical communication gap between **Ethical Security Researchers**, **Financial Enterprises**, and **Law Enforcement Agencies**.

In the current landscape, cybercrime investigations are siloed and slow. Researchers find vulnerabilities that get ignored, enterprises suffer breaches they can't effectively report, and police lack the technical context to prosecute. H3M4 unifies these stakeholders into a single, synchronous, and active platform where intelligence is shared instantly, verified by consensus, and converted into immutable forensic evidence.

---

## 🏛️ The Three Pillars of H3M4

### 1. 🕵️ The Research Commons (The "Finders")
*   **Role:** Ethical hackers and security researchers submit vulnerabilities and threat intelligence.
*   **Innovation:** A **Reputation Engine** validates their work. Instead of just "bug bounties," researches earn "Intelligence Credits" and establish a verifiable track record.
*   **Key Feature:** **Live Intel Feed** & **Zero-Day Disclosure Protocol** that warns the ecosystem before attacks spread.

### 2. 🏢 The Enterprise Shield (The "Defenders")
*   **Role:** Banks, FinTechs, and Corporations that need protection.
*   **Innovation:** **Automated Threat Mapping**. When an enterprise is attacked, the system automatically correlates the attack signature with known researcher submissions.
*   **Key Feature:** **Victim Vault**, allowing companies to securely share incident logs with police without exposing sensitive customer data publicly, using Zero-Knowledge proofs.

### 3. 🚓 The Enforcement Node (The "Enforcers")
*   **Role:** Police and Cyber Crime Investigation Cells.
*   **Innovation:** **"Attached Intelligence"**. Officers investigating a case don't just see a "Complaint ID"; they see the exact technical weapon used, the researcher who analyzed it, and the victim's logs—all bundled together.
*   **Key Feature:** **Official Share**, a secure, ledger-tracked system to share case files with other agencies and prosecutors, ensuring chain-of-custody is never broken.

---

## ⭐ Key Capabilities
*   **Synchronous Intelligence:** A threat finding submitted by a researcher updates the Admin's graph and the Police's case file in real-time.
*   **Immutable Evidence:** leveraging blockchain/ledger concepts to ensure that once evidence (logs/reports) is sealed, it cannot be tampered with, making it "Court-Ready."
*   **Visual Threat Graph:** An interactive, real-time visualization of how threats, actors, and targets are connected.

## 🛠️ Technology Stack
*   **Frontend:** React, TypeScript, TailwindCSS, ShadCN UI (Glassmorphism Design).
*   **Backend:** Node.js, Express.
*   **Data & State:** PostgreSQL (Drizzle ORM), React Query for real-time synchronization.
*   **Visualization:** Framer Motion, Recharts for dynamic threat graphs.
